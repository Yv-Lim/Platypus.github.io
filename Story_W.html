Platypus is very happy!

Purr...! Thank you for the pat! My fur seems even smoother! In return I want to tell you a story.

Platypus:

One day a duck got lost and met a water rat.
They fell in love and gave birth to what is now known as me, the Platypus.
I share the fur of my dad, the water rat, and the bill and webbed feet of my mum, the duck.
Thanks for listening to my story, here is a clue to find Koala:
Koala can be found near its favourite gum trees,
But it was tricked and is quite displeased,
The leaves are there but Koala can't eat,
look for the mural just along Bowen street.
